# Ardy Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** August 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Ardy ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Ardy mobile application and related services (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

**Account information.** When you register, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and password (stored as a secure hash, never in plain text). You select a role — buyer, seller, or owner.

**Listing content.** If you post a listing, we collect the title, property type, price, size, location, description, and any photos you upload.

**Land Advisor conversations.** When you use the Land Advisor chat feature, we collect the messages you send, the assistant's responses, and any stated preferences (e.g. property type, budget, location) used to tailor recommendations. These messages are sent to our AI provider, OpenAI, to generate responses (see Section 3).

**Favorites.** We record which listings you save as favorites.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the information above to: operate your account and authenticate you; display and manage listings; power the Land Advisor chat assistant, including generating conversational responses and matching listings to your stated preferences; and maintain your favorites.

We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

## 3. Third-Party Service Providers

We share limited data with the following third parties, solely to provide the App's functionality:

- **OpenAI** — processes the text of your Land Advisor conversations and generates vector embeddings of listing descriptions, in order to power the chat assistant and improve listing search/matching. OpenAI's own privacy policy and data-use terms govern how it handles this data on our behalf.
- **Cloudinary** — stores and serves the photos you upload with your listings.

Neither provider is authorized to use your data for purposes beyond providing services to us.

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your personal information with other users beyond what is inherent to the App's function (e.g., your listings and name may be visible to other users as part of normal marketplace use). We may disclose information if required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Ardy, our users, or the public.

## 5. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encrypted password storage and encrypted data transmission (HTTPS). No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your account, listing, conversation, and favorites data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time (Section 7).

## 7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at [insert contact email]. We will respond to verified requests within a reasonable timeframe, subject to any legal obligations to retain certain records.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect data from children under that age. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Our service providers, including OpenAI, may process data on servers located outside your country of residence. By using the App, you consent to this transfer and processing.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a new effective date. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
